# Create a Non Cash Item Order

This section explains how to create a new Non Cash Item Order, review the order, complete the Crew Sign Off process, and cancel the order if required.

---

## Step 1: Navigate to Non Cash Item

From the main navigation menu, select **Non Cash Item**.

![Step 1](/img/non-cash-item/step-01.jpeg)

---

## Step 2: Start a New Order

Click **Start New Order** to begin creating a new Non Cash Item Order.

![Step 2](/img/non-cash-item/step-02.jpeg)

---

## Step 3: Select the Order Type

Choose either **Scheduled Delivery** or **Ad-Hoc Delivery** depending on your business requirements.

:::note

The remaining steps are the same regardless of the selected order type.

:::

![Step 3](/img/non-cash-item/step-03.jpeg)

---

## Step 4: Select Non Cash Items

Choose the required non-cash items that you want to include in the order.

![Step 4](/img/non-cash-item/step-04.jpeg)

---

## Step 5: Enter Item Quantities

Specify the required quantity for each selected item.

![Step 5](/img/non-cash-item/step-05.jpeg)

---

## Step 6: Select Delivery Location and Date

Choose the destination location and the required delivery date.

![Step 6](/img/non-cash-item/step-06.jpeg)

---

## Step 7: Continue to Review

Click **Next** to review your order.

![Step 7](/img/non-cash-item/step-07.jpeg)

---

## Step 8: Review the Order

Verify that all selected items, quantities, and delivery information are correct.

![Step 8](/img/non-cash-item/step-08.jpeg)

---

## Step 9: Confirm the Order

Click **Confirm** to submit the Non Cash Item Order.

![Step 9](/img/non-cash-item/step-09.jpeg)

---

## Step 10: Order Successfully Created

Your order has now been created successfully.

From this screen you can:

- View the order
- Print the confirmation
- Complete Crew Sign Off
- Cancel the order

![Step 10](/img/non-cash-item/step-10.jpeg)

---

# View Order Confirmation

## Step 11: Open the Order

Locate the newly created order and click **View**.

![Step 11](/img/non-cash-item/step-11.jpeg)

---

## Step 12: Review the Confirmation

Review the complete order details and confirmation information.

![Step 12](/img/non-cash-item/step-12.jpeg)

---

# Crew Sign Off

## Step 13: Open Crew Sign Off

Click **Crew Sign Off**.

![Step 13](/img/non-cash-item/step-13.jpeg)

---

## Step 14: Verify OTP and QR Code

Use the displayed OTP or QR Code to complete the Crew Sign Off process.

![Step 14](/img/non-cash-item/step-14.jpeg)

---

# Cancel a Non Cash Item Order

## Step 15: Cancel the Order

Click **Cancel Order**.

![Step 15](/img/non-cash-item/step-15.jpeg)

---

## Step 16: Confirm Cancellation

Confirm that you want to cancel the order.

:::warning

Cancelled orders cannot be processed unless a new order is created.

:::

![Step 16](/img/non-cash-item/step-16.jpeg)

---

## Step 17: Return to the Order List

Click **Back to List** to return to the order listing.

![Step 17](/img/non-cash-item/step-17.jpeg)

---

## Step 18: Verify the Order Status

Locate the order and verify that its status has been updated.

![Step 18](/img/non-cash-item/step-18.jpeg)

---

## Summary

You have successfully learned how to:

- Create a Non Cash Item Order
- Review and confirm the order
- View the order confirmation
- Complete Crew Sign Off
- Cancel an order when required
