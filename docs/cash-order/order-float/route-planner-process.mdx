# Assign Float Orders to Runs

:::tip

These steps are performed by the **Route Planner**.

:::

## Step 16: Open Order Management

Login using the Route Planner role.

Navigate to **Order Management**.

Choose:

- Assigned for Scheduled Orders
- Unassigned for Ad-Hoc Orders

![Step 16](/img/float-order/step-16.jpeg)

---

## Step 17: Move Orders to Runs

Select the orders.

Click **Move to Runs**.

Confirm the action.

![Step 17](/img/float-order/step-17.jpeg)

---

## Step 18: Open Run Assignment

Choose the newly created Run.

![Step 18](/img/float-order/step-18.jpeg)

---

## Step 19: Verify Orders

Review the orders assigned to the Run.

Click **Move**.

![Step 19](/img/float-order/step-19.jpeg)

---

## Step 20: Configure the Runsheet

Choose:

- Service Date
- User
- Vehicle

![Step 20](/img/float-order/step-20.jpeg)

---

## Step 21: Runsheet Assigned

The orders are now assigned to the Runsheet.

![Step 21](/img/float-order/step-21.jpeg)

---

## Step 22: View Order Status

Click the **Settings** icon to view the order status.

![Step 22](/img/float-order/step-22.jpeg)

---

## Step 23: Delete Runsheet (Optional)

If required, delete the Runsheet using the Delete action.

![Step 23](/img/float-order/step-23.jpeg)

:::warning

Deleting a Runsheet may affect scheduled deliveries. Ensure the Runsheet is no longer required before removing it.

:::
