# Cash Collection: CIT Portal Assignment

This guide covers **Phase 1-3** for managing Cash In Transit (CIT) portal assignments, filtering orders, and creating runsheets.

:::tip 
Prerequisites
Ensure you have the necessary **Dispatcher** or **Operations Manager** permissions before attempting to assign runs.
:::

---

## Phase 1: Locating & Filtering Orders

### Step 1: Access the Portal
Open the **CIT Portal** and log in with your credentials.

![Step 27](../static/img/cash-collection/cit/step-27.jpeg)

### Step 2: Navigate to Orders and Apply Filters
From the main sidebar navigation dashboard, go to <span class="badge badge--secondary">Order Management</span> and apply the relevant filters to locate the desired order(s).
Use the date picker tool to apply the relevant **Date Filter** based on your target pickup date.

![Step 30](../static/img/cash-collection/cit/step-30.jpeg)

### Step 3: View the Records
After assigning the filters you can see the records like this. One is the <span class="badge badge--warning">Unassigned</span> order and the other is the <span class="badge badge--success">Assigned</span> order.

## Phase 2: Creating and Assigning the Run

### Step 1: Select and Move Order
Check the box next to the desired order(s) and click the **Move** button.

![Step 32](../static/img/cash-collection/cit/step-32.jpeg)

### Step 2: Allocate to a Run
From the modal prompt, choose the destination to move the selected order into an active **Run**.

![Step 33](../static/img/cash-collection/cit/step-33.jpeg)



{/* ### Step 3: Open Run Assignment
Navigate to the <span class="badge badge--primary">Run Assignment</span> tab and apply your filters to find your recently moved run.

![Step 34](../static/img/cash-collection/cit/step-34.jpeg) */}



### Step 4: Select the Active Run
Navigate to the <span class="badge badge--primary">Run Assignment</span> tab and apply your filters to find your recently moved run.
Locate and select the specific **Run** from the filtered list and select **Move**.

![Step 35](../static/img/cash-collection/cit/step-35.jpeg)

### Step 5: Assign User, Vehicle & Generate Runsheet
Assign the appropriate **User** (Driver/Guard) and **Vehicle**, then click the **Move to Runsheet** action button.

![Step 36](../static/img/cash-collection/cit/step-36.jpeg)

## Phase 3: Managing the Runsheet

:::success 
Runsheet Created Successfully
The runsheet is now live and can be accessed by field personnel or drivers assigned to the vehicle.
:::

### Step 1: Confirmation
Review the newly created **Runsheet** details on your screen to ensure asset fields are correct.

![Step 37](../static/img/cash-collection/cit/step-37.jpeg)
